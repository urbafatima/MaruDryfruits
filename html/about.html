About us

Welcome to Maru Dry Fruits

For over 40 years, Maru Dry Fruits has been a trusted provider of premium dry fruits to customers across the nation. Our wide range of premium quality includes nuts, dry fruits, chocolate, honey, dates and snacks. We at Maru Dry Fruits carefully source and hand-pick items to ensure the freshest and tastiest products for our customers. We are a trusted name in the market committed to delivering excellence and satisfaction to our valued customers. Among our dried fruit selection, you'll find some of the best-tasting and most nutritious options. They are all carefully dried to retain their natural flavors and nutrients. These dried fruits are perfect for snacking, baking, or adding to your breakfast cereal.


When it comes to imported dates or chocolates, we gather the highest quality from around the world. You'll be pleased to know that we import dates from Saudi Arabia which includes Ajwa, Kalmi, Mabroom, Khudri, Sukhri etc. On the other hand we offer Medjoul dates from Jordan. These two countries are famously known for producing some of the best dates in the world. These imported dates are sweet, juicy, and flavorful. For celebrations, appreciation or corporate gifting, the dry fruit baskets are a thoughtful gift. These gift baskets are healthy and tasty treats which include specific types of dry fruits, nuts, and chocolates.


We offer a variety of pre-made baskets to choose from, and also provide the option to customize the baskets according to your budget and preferences. We offer dry Strawberry, dry Kiwi slice, dry Blueberry, dry Mango slice, Raisins, Figs, dry Dates, dry Apricot, and many more. They are all carefully dried to retain their natural flavors and nutrients. These dried fruits are perfect for snacking, baking, or adding to your breakfast cereal.
